Middleton Trail Map

This is my first cartography project outside of classwork and the UWCL. The Town of Middleton Parks & Rec reached out to Madison area cartographer and requested a new trail map in the summer of 2020. Several people, including myself, agreed to work on the project.

Tasks

The Parks Commission of the Town of Middleton gave project workers three tasks:

1.) Create a set of trail maps for public usage

2.) Create a tool or a recommendation for the parks commission to plan trails in the future

3.) Compile coordinates of trailheads and park entrances for the Town

The Park Commission chair elaborated on these tasks, stating that the Town did not necessarily need all of the work to be completed by project workers.
