Christopher Archuleta

I, Christopher Archuleta, am a student cartographer at the University of Wisconsin-Madison. My main objective is to have a satisfying and impactful cartography career. As of December 2019, I am working toward this goal by studying cartography at the University and doing projects for the UW Cartography Lab (UWCL) and its clients. The purpose of this web page is to display some of my works, methods, activities, and networks for visitors like you to see. If you have any reason to contact me, please do so.

Making a Difference: Madison

This map is a slight revision of my final project for the Introduction to Cartography course at UW-Madison, which Robert Roth taught when I took it in Fall 2018.

A UW student organization that helps the homeless in Madison meet their basic needs, which I am a part of, uses this map at informational meetings to show members the importance of the org's outreach component. More details on the map's design process, data sources, and future are to come.
